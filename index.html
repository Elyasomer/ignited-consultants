Introduction

As educators we recognise the importance of lifelong learning and its benefits. Education not only prepares young people for their future roles but gives them choices. We applaud teachers and those passionate about education, who are committed to changes which enable them to become future leaders. We applaud the mechanisms that helps to create them and their dedication to their goals.

As consultants it is our job to support schools, and to keep teachers motivated.. We will support you with building protocols, establishing strategies for growth, and developing supportive systems for student and staff well-being.

Children are at the heart of what we do, and is our intention to develop the important relationship between them and their teachers, so that all can succeed.

Part of our role is to support and guide parents, in their decision-making process. We offer advice that will ensure that the chosen school or programme is a good personal choice for your child, and that as a result, the student and you, the parent witness both academic and personal growth.

Our Aims

We aim to relieve you of the difficulties in negotiating your way through the multilayered processes for admission into a school programme, and to guide you through the testing stages that you will encounter. Your interests become our interests

IGNITED helps schools to uncover their greatest opportunities for growth, development and achievement. We work side by side with our clients to build programmes and policies that will propel the school forward, build on student success and to support the needs of the staff. Our ultimate aim is to ensure that your school performs well and that the staff are fully supported in their professional way.

We can call upon our technical experts to address any issues that you may have in accessing or completing online application forms. They will also be aware of the latest technologies and resources that can be used in the classroom,

Let us know your thoughts and needs. We can work together and help you face your challenges and promote your opportunities. Together we can help you to thrive. Contact us and we can start the journey.
